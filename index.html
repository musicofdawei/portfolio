SCAD summer program, first digital animation
I never had access to a drawing tablet before, and had held off on buying one despite how much digital art interested me because I was worried about how the feel would disrupt my usual “flow” when drawing. I had attempted digital art before with a mousepad, so that was my only experience to go off of. I found digital art with a tablet to be much more gratifying than what I originally envisioned, and I also learned a lot about animation for having essentially no experience with it in this medium. This is the end product of 12 hours of work. Made on toon boom.
